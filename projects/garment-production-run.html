Toko Tania Home Textiles

Bed sheets, curtains, bedding, and interior fabrics for a family-run home decor retailer — in production since 1991.

Project Overview

Toko Tania is a family-owned interior and decoration business established in 1991, offering premium home decor at accessible prices. They needed a manufacturing partner to produce the full range of textile products they sell — from bed sheets and custom bedding to curtains, upholstery fabrics, and related home furnishings.

BantuProduksi handles ongoing production across their catalog: TENCEL and cotton bedding sets, King Koil fabric lines, Japanese and local cotton collections, canvas and vitrage fabrics, curtain panels, upholstery materials, and custom-made bedding to order. We manage fabric sourcing, cutting, sewing, finishing, labeling, and quality control so Toko Tania can focus on retail and customer service.

What We Delivered

  • Bed sheets and bedding sets — TENCEL, King Koil, Japanese cotton, and local cotton
  • Curtains, canvas, vitrage, and upholstery fabrics cut and sewn to spec
  • Custom bedding made to customer measurements and fabric choices
  • Bulk production, finishing, folding, and packaging ready for shop and online sales
