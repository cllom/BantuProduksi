CNC Wood Furniture

Flat-pack desk and shelving units for a co-working operator — 350 sets across two locations.

Project Overview

A co-working space operator expanding in Bali and Yogyakarta needed durable, brand-consistent desk and shelving units that could ship flat and assemble on site. BantuProduksi managed the full MaaS workflow — CAD optimization for CNC nesting, local plywood sourcing, precision routing, edge banding, and kit packaging.

The client's interior designer supplied reference dimensions and finish samples. We converted those into production-ready toolpaths, produced a full-scale mock-up for approval, and delivered 350 flat-pack sets with labeled hardware bags and illustrated assembly guides in Bahasa Indonesia and English.

What We Delivered

  • CNC routing of 18 mm birch plywood with optimized sheet nesting
  • Edge banding, sanding, and clear matte lacquer finish
  • Flat-pack kitting with hardware, labels, and bilingual assembly guides
  • Staged delivery to two co-working locations on a rolling schedule
