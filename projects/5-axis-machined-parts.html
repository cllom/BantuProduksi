5-Axis Machined Parts

Complex aluminum housings for an aerospace sub-tier supplier — 800 units with CMM verification.

Project Overview

An aerospace sub-tier supplier needed contoured aluminum housings with tight positional tolerances and minimal setup changes across a medium-volume run. BantuProduksi provided full MaaS coverage — material certification, 5-axis programming, in-process probing, CMM inspection, and anodized surface finishing — from our metal fabrication partners in Indonesia.

The client supplied IGES models and a detailed quality plan. We machined first-article parts, submitted full dimensional reports, and after approval produced 800 units in three release lots. Complementary laser-cut sheet metal inserts were nested and finished in parallel to reduce overall lead time.

What We Delivered

  • 5-axis CNC machining of 6061-T6 aluminum housings from certified billet
  • Fiber laser cutting of internal bracket and cover plate sub-assemblies
  • Type II anodizing, deburring, and CMM inspection per client quality plan
  • Lot traceability, FAIR documentation, and phased delivery over 12 weeks
