About BantuProduksi

Produksi mudah, usaha maju.

Our Vision

We exist to help businesses grow — by giving them the power to create almost anything their city consumes, without needing to own a factory.

Who We Are

BantuProduksi is a full-service manufacturing partner based in Indonesia. We combine textiles, metals, plastics, wood, electronics, and digital fabrication under one roof — so entrepreneurs, brands, and institutions can go from idea to finished product with a single team they trust.

Think of us the way the tech world thinks of TSMC: NVIDIA, Intel, AMD, and Apple design the chips — TSMC fabricates them at scale. You bring the vision, brand, and market. We bring the production floor, engineering, and quality control.

What You Focus On

Your business should spend its energy on what creates the most value — product design, customer relationships, marketing, distribution, and growth. BantuProduksi handles the rest: prototyping, sourcing, machining, sewing, assembly, testing, and delivery.

  • Design and brand — yours
  • Sales, partnerships, and go-to-market — yours
  • Manufacturing, fabrication, and scale-up — ours

Building Capability — Not Just Products

We do more than fill orders. We help businesses develop real research and product-creation capability — so they are not locked into off-the-shelf goods forever. Whether you need a first prototype, a small batch, or volume production, we work alongside your team through development, iteration, and launch.

What We Make

From uniforms and home textiles to precision metal parts, custom furniture, automated architectural systems, and PCB-driven prototypes — if a city buys it, we can likely help you build it. Our portfolio spans hospitality, retail, architecture, electronics, and industrial supply chains across Indonesia.

Educating the Next Generation

Digital fabrication is changing how products are made. We are committed to educating young people — students, makers, and early-career engineers — about CNC, 3D printing, electronics, textiles, and modern production workflows so the next generation can design, build, and lead industries of their own.

Ready to Build?

Whether you are launching a new product line or scaling something that already sells, we are here to manufacture it.
